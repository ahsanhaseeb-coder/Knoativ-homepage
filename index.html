04 / How we work

Three weeks.
Three milestones.
No discovery theater.

We don't run two-month discovery phases that produce slide decks. We diagnose, architect, and ship — in that order, with the same senior person in the room from start to finish.
Phase 01 Week 1

Diagnose what's actually broken.

Senior person on site or on call. We map the operational reality — not what the deck says, what the team actually does. No questionnaires. No discovery workshops.

  • Operational mapping
  • Constraint identification
  • Stakeholder alignment
Phase 02 Week 2

Architect the smallest working system.

We design the minimum system that solves the problem in production — not the maximum system that justifies the invoice. Scope is the discipline.

  • System architecture
  • Integration plan
  • Deployment timeline
Phase 03 Weeks 3+

Build it. Ship it. Stay in the room.

We build, deploy to production, and stay accountable post-ship. The senior person who scoped it is the senior person who hands it over to your team.

  • Production build
  • Live deployment
  • Operator handover
